Where do our mangoes come from?

Originating from Southeast Asia, it has this delicious one fruit spread worldwide. The journey of a mango begins as a small flower on the branches of the mango tree. After Through pollination, the flower grows into a small, green fruit that ripens under the warm sun. Experienced pickers climb deftly in the trees, carefully harvesting and sorting the mangoes for transport to markets around the world world. Like this the mango completes its remarkable journey, from orchard to fruit bowl, admired for its exoticness taste and nutritional value.
